# Privacy and store launch checklist

This is an engineering checklist, not legal advice. Launch-market counsel must
approve the final policy, DPA, consent language and recording rules.

- Document controller/processor roles, purposes, lawful bases, regions,
  transfers, retention, deletion and data-subject request channels.
- Publish an approved subprocessor list covering hosting, identity, email,
  storage, transcription, billing, crash reporting and push notifications.
- Capture versioned organization authorization and employee acknowledgement;
  recording upload remains disabled without the required disclosure evidence.
- Exercise access/export, correction, consent withdrawal and deletion, including
  objects, derivatives, transcripts, indexes and backups under documented SLAs.
- Complete Play Data safety and App Store privacy labels from actual enabled
  code paths. Generic push notifications must reveal no sensitive lock-screen data.
- Document Android call-log distribution approval and iOS cellular-history
  limitation. Regional dialer capabilities remain separately feature-flagged.
- Counsel, security, product and privacy owners sign dated release evidence.
